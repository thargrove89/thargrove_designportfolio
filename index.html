About

My name is Travis Hargrove and I'm a graphic designer and web developer located in Greenville, NC. I attended the University of East Carolina where I studied information technology. I have studied all facets of web development. From backend technologies like PHP and SQL to frontend technologies HTML, CSS, and Javascript. However, I specialize in graphic design.I love creating visually interesting things using color, shape, and typography.

All of my designs are simple, clean, and straight to the point. I can design a wide range of products like web banners, infographics, logos, flyers, and brochures just to name a few. When I am not designing or researching the latest design trends, I enjoy spending time with my beautiful wife, watching movies, running and reading biographies about great people in history.
